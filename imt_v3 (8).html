ⓘ What happens at site level
▼
  • Site IC manages response and reports to Group IMT if escalation occurs
  • Belinga Monitoring Centre provides situational feed during active incidents
  • Site-level regulatory notifications (WorkSafe WA) may still be required at this level
  • Group HS&EM notified of all significant site incidents regardless of escalation

IMT Roles
Level 3 - Crisis Management Team
Who activates
IC recommends. Any IMT member can raise the flag.
Notification
Text, phone, and email to CMT members simultaneously
IMT continues
CMT does not replace the IMT. IC reports to CMT Chair. Operational control stays with IMT.
When to activate the CMT
Fatality or multiple serious injuries
Significant environmental release triggering regulator notification
ASX material disclosure threshold reached
Significant or likely media and reputational exposure
Multi-site or production-impacting event
Government or ministerial engagement required
Spend or resource authority exceeds IMT delegation
🔄 How escalation works
▼
1
IC recommends CMT activation. Any IMT member can flag the need.
2
CMT notified via text, phone, email. Assembles at Tea Tree Level 1 or joins IMT Teams channel.
3
IMT continues operating. CMT does not take over the room.
4
CMT handles Board/government engagement, ASX notifications, decisions above IMT delegation.
5
IC briefs CMT at regular intervals using the Incident Action Plan and situation reports.

CMT composition: CEO or delegate, CFO, General Counsel, CPO, and relevant Group GM. Confirmed on activation.
